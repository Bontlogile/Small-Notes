Role of the Senior Financial Manager

The role and responsibility of the senior financial Executive

Financial management is the use of resources well to meet objectives. Often, profit maximization is incorrectly assumed to be the main objective of the business; this is usually not so. For a profit making company, a better objective is the maximization of shareholder wealth. Reasons why maximization of shareholder wealth is the better objective is because takes into consideration the future the dividends financial plans and risk management as the intergral component of its approach.
